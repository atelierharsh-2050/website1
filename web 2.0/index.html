About us

Harsh Atelier is a branding and marketing studio focused on heritage and slow fashion brands. The studio helps transform meaningful craftsmanship into strong, modern brands by building clear identities, creating digital presence, and guiding brands through thoughtful marketing. While aesthetics are important, the work goes beyond how a brand looks. Every design, strategy, and marketing effort is shaped in the right direction to help brands reach the right audience and drive real sales.

Across India, countless crafts carry generations of skill, culture, and meaning. The craftsmanship is exceptional, the materials are rare, and the process often takes days or even weeks. Yet without clear branding and modern presence, these creations remain invisible in the market.

The goal is simple: build brands that honor craft, culture, and long-term value

The Bengal Cat Bengal cat is one of Bengal’s most underrated symbols. Despite its elegance and remarkable presence, many people are still unfamiliar with this extraordinary breed. Known for its refined appearance and rare beauty, the Bengal cat is considered one of the most exclusive and expensive cat breeds in the world. Its elegance, rarity, and distinctive character naturally reflect ideas of luxury and exclusivity, while its origins remain deeply connected to Bengal. This is why the Bengal cat became the symbol of Harsh Atelier — a studio based in Kolkata, choosing an emblem that reflects both its cultural roots and its connection to Benga
